Spacecubed

Highly involved with Spacecubed for over 8 years, I contributed to establishing one of the leading co-working communities within the region, whilst directly contributing to the growth of startup and social enterprise ecosystem in Western Australia.

As Global Head of Strategic Projects, I have a new opportunity to connect closely with business owners from across the world. I have spearheaded the development of our digital platform, and am the main point of contact for all space partnerships across the business.

My main focus has been exploring alternate management models through national partnerships, and shifting our focus online with our in-house software solutions.
